POLITICAL

Establishing a Public Transit System

Transportation is the largest source of air pollution across the world. Public transit can help to drastically reduce the amount of air pollution that vehicles release into the atmosphere, which can drastically reduce your carbon footprint if you ride one of those vehicles.

Carbon Policing

The Government could impose “carbon taxes and cap-and-trade programs” that could exercise an amount of control over the limitation of carbon emission produced by businesses with special permits “for each ton (of carbon) they emit”, and taxes for “each ton of greenhouse gases they (the businesses) emit”.

Increase Energy Efficiency

Energy efficiency is the ability to reduce the energy spent without affecting its quality of use. The government should offer tax breaks to utilities that could improve energy efficiency to various “consumer products” (such as vehicles, appliances, and housings).

ECONOMIC

Landfill recycling

Decomposition produce harmful methane emissions that damages the environment. Companies such as “Renergy” could partner with municipalities and food manufacturers to redirect their organic waste away from landfills and help reduce carbon emissions.

Production of Renewable Energy

More than 50 percent of electricity in the United States comes from polluting coal-fired power plants. Renewable energy sources like solar, wind and biomass reduce air pollution and generate no greenhouse gas emissions from fossil fuels.

Telecommunication

Businesses should implement telecommunication meetings such as Zoom, Skype, or Microsoft Team instead of having to physically meet at a centralized location, which requires transportation to that location. Less transportation would mean less energy wasted on travel.

Green Homes

A positive thing we can all do for our communities is to adopt eco-friendly practices at home by planting a native garden, using non-toxic household products, or turning the thermostat down three degrees in the winter and up three degrees in the summer to reduce CO2 emissions.

Buy Organic

Buying plant-based, unprocessed foods from local farmer’s markets, green restaurants, or health food stores reduces the carbon footprint that you produce.

Turn It Off!

By just making sure that you are turning off the lights after you're done using a room before you go to bed, and before you leave the house. Doing this reduces the amount of electricity that your house needs to be using at that exact time, and it reduces your electricity bill

Carbon Offset

Carbon offset is a global method of reducing carbon release in the atmosphere through investments in renewable energy projects and incrementing carbon storage through land restoration and planting trees. Businesses should invest in carbon offsets by funding “transparent” projects.
