WHAT IS CARBON FOOTPRINT?

Carbon footprint is the total greenhouse gas emissions from commercial and professional usage expressed as carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2).

WHY DO WE NEED A SOLUTION FOR CO2?

Carbon emissions have a tremendous effect on the planet, as greenhouse gases produce the highest levels of emissions in the atmosphere. If it is left untreated then it will cause global warming and climate change.

HOW CAN THIS AFFECT US?

As more fossil fuels are burned, carbon dioxide is released into the atmosphere that causes global warming and as the globe is warming, even by a small percentage, there starts to be a lot of climate change starting to happen
